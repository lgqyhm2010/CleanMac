# Commercialization Guardrails

CleanMac should not monetize by weakening the trust path. The core cleanup loop stays available and visible:

- AI review remains available for selected candidates.
- User-visible safety rules remain visible before cleanup.
- Protected candidates remain blocked from Move to Trash.
- Cleanup continues to move files to Trash instead of permanently deleting them.

Paid packaging can be considered later for convenience features such as scheduled scans, richer history, team presets, or advanced automation. It should not hide why an item was selected, skip review, remove protected-item safeguards, or make permanent deletion the default.
